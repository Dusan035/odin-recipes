Goulash

Goulash, Serbian style delicacy

This is recipee for traditional goulash

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic

Recipee instruction

  1. stew onions and meat first with a little oil
  2. add mushrooms and black pepper and water
  3. cook for 3 hours
  4. add garlic
