Sarma

Sarma, the Serbian delicacy!

This recipe for Serbian stuffed cabbage, or sarma

Ingredients:

  • 1 (3-to 4-pound) head cabbage
  • 1kg of ground meat
  • bacon
  • onions
  • smoked ribs
  • tomato sauce
  • black pepper
  • rice

Recipee instruction:

  1. Steam the head of cabbage until the outer leaves are limp, then cool it and separate the leaves
  2. In a bowl mix togather rice, meat and seasoning
  3. Fill cabbage leaves with the mix previously prepared and roll it
  4. add tomato sauce, ribs and bacon into the bowl and cook
